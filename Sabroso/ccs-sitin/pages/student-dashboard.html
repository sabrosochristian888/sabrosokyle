Rules and Regulations

  3. Respect lab equipment and avoid moving devices without permission.
  4. Keep noise to a minimum and avoid disturbing other students.
  5. Follow the instructor or lab assistant instructions at all times.
  6. Eat and drink are not allowed inside the lab unless explicitly permitted.
  7. Report any issues or damages immediately to the lab administrator.
  8. End your sit-in session promptly when your work is completed.
